Douglas' Biography

Douglas is 41 years old and the father of three children, including me. Even with a life full of challenges, he has always been a symbol of strength and dedication.

In his youth, Douglas was very close to becoming a professional soccer player. It was a dream within reach, but he stepped away from it due to a personal relationship.

Currently, Douglas works with an inflatable balloon attraction at the town square in Mangabeiras. With his cheerful personality, he brings joy to children and families and is well loved in the community.

He is no longer with my mother, but that never stopped him from being present in our lives. He has always fought to provide the best for us, even during tough times. His affection, courage, and companionship make him a remarkable father.
