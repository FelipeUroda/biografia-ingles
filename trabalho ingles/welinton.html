biografia Marcos Vinicios

Marcos Vinicios carreiro sousa was born on 12 september 2006,
in São Raimundo das Mangabeiras, the son of José Airton da Silva Sousa
e Elessandra Alves Carreiro, ele estudou naescola José Pinto de Menezes.

At the age of 4 marcos was abandoned by his mother at the door of
his house and went to live with his father and stepmother Ana maria in
the countryside of mangabeiras.


when he was in the second year of primary achool, his father held
him back in the second grade. Marcos grew up working, helping his father
on the farmand whit cattle, and after work, he still went school. When
he turned 15 he moved in whit his mother in goiania and had to transfer
from his school to a school in goiania. when he tried to enroll in his oud
school but there were issues whit his documents, and he had to stop´studying
for a while until the next year to restart classes.

At 17, he went to live with his grandmother Maria Domingas and his grandfather
Pedro Lopes de Sousa because he did not want to continue working on the farm and with
cattle. At 18, he took the EJA exam and managed to pass it. Today, he studies at
São Raimundo Nonato school and is enrolled in the EJA programme.
