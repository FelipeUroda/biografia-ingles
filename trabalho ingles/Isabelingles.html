Biography of João Santos de Arruda

Biography: João Santos de Arruda

Basic Information

  • Full Name: João Santos de Arruda
  • Birth Date: March 25, 1990
  • Place of Birth: Grajaú - MA, Brazil
  • Age: 35 years old
  • Marital Status: Married since 2011
  • Children: Two daughters

His Story

João was born in the city of Grajaú and had a very good childhood.

He studied at Major Altino school and enjoyed participating in local church festivals. He didn’t grow up in a rich family, but he was happy and learned to value life.

He became a farmer and worked hard every day. One of his dreams was to get his driver's license.

It wasn’t easy, because he had to travel every day from Formosa da Serra Negra to Grajaú, where the driving school was. But he didn’t give up and finally got it.

He bought some land and built a family. His biggest inspirations were his mother and father.

João didn’t waste time — every morning, he woke up early, thanked God, and worked hard to build a better future.

He didn’t want to live in big cities, but he visited nearby towns and even traveled to Tocantins.

For him, family, friends, and religion were the most important things in life.

Beliefs and Legacy

He believed in loving God, protecting his family, and helping others.

He wished the retirement system for elders had started earlier in Brazil. One of the most special days in his life was his wedding.
