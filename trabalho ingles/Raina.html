Byography of Tainá Conceição Sousa

Tainá Conceição Sousa was born on June 21, 2006, in the city of Balsas, Maranhão, Brazil. Since childhood, she has lived in São Raimundo das Mangabeiras, where she grew up and built her life.

She was always dedicated and committed to everything she did. During her childhood and teenage years,she shared many special moments with her sister, with whom she was raised and developed a strong bond of companionship and true friendship.

Tainá was never afraid to dream big. She started studying Veterinary Medicine, following her passion for animals and her desire to care for their health and well-being. Besides her studies, she stood out as the coordinator of the Youth Ministry (PJ) in Mangabeiras, where she led with enthusiasm and inspired many young people in the community.

She did not give up when facing obstacles and always showed strength and determination. Her presence in the community was marked by the love, responsibility, and dedication she brought to everything she was involved in.

Tainá is special not only for her achievements but for being a beloved sister, a young leader, and an example of commitment to the future and to others.
