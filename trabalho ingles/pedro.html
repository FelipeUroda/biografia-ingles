Biography – Leila Ernandes

Leila Ernandes was born in 1991 in Morro do Chupé, a small village in the municipality of São Raimundo das Mangabeiras, Brazil. She was raised mainly by her father, because her mother left when she was still a child. Her childhood was marked by simplicity, responsibility, and strong family values.

At the age of 14, Leila got married. One year later, at 15, she started working on a farm as a cook. It was a tough job for someone so young, but she worked hard and learned fast. At 16, she became a mother for the first time.

Over the years, she continued to work and take care of her family. Her dedication and skill in the kitchen helped her grow professionally. At the age of 31, she had her second child. Today, Leila is 34 years old and works as a cook in a restaurant in the same city where she was born and raised.

Leila is a strong woman, admired by those around her for her perseverance and love for her children. Her story is a true example of courage and determination.
